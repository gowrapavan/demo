About NARAYANA JR. COLLEGE BRUNDAVAN GARDEN GUNTUR

Established in 2012 and managed by Pvt. Unaided, NARAYANA JR. COLLEGE BRUNDAVAN GARDEN GUNTUR is situated in an Urban area within the GUNTUR block of GUNTUR district, ANDHRA PRADESH. The college offers Grades 11 to 12 and operates as a Co-educational institution without a pre-primary section. Instruction is provided in English, and the academic session commences in April.

The college boasts N/A building infrastructure, comprising 0 classrooms in good condition. It also features 2 rooms for non-teaching activities and a separate room for the Headmaster/Teacher. However, there is no boundary wall or electric connection. Drinking water is sourced externally, and sanitation facilities include 0 functional boys’ toilets and 0 girls’ toilets. While there is no playground or library, the college does not require ramps for disabled access. Furthermore, computer facilities and mid-day meals are not provided.
