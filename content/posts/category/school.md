## About Dr. Kondabolu Lakshmi Prasad Public School, Guntur

Dr. Kondabolu Lakshmi Prasad Public School, also known as Dr. KLP Public School, was established in 1983. It is managed by the Nagarjuna Education Society and is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).

## Academics

The Dr. Kondabolu Lakshmi Prasad Public School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers a diverse range of streams including PCM, PCB, and Arts. The medium of instruction is English, catering to students from Nursery to 12th grade. It follows a co-educational system and operates as a boarding school with 87 classrooms. The school, categorized as unaided/independent, provides education up to Senior Secondary level and was established in 2005.


## PRIMARY TO SECONDARY

We follow the NCERT syllabus and curriculum from Grade I to XII, with a special focus on Artificial Intelligence for Classes IX & X.
